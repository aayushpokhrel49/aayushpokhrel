About Aayushi

Aayushi is an intelligent search engine that combines the power of traditional web search with cutting-edge artificial intelligence to deliver the most relevant and comprehensive results.

AI-Powered Summaries: Get concise, accurate summaries of search results powered by our advanced language model.
Lightning Fast: Experience ultra-fast search results with our optimized search algorithms.
Privacy Focused: We respect your privacy and don't track your searches or personal data.
Beautiful Interface: Enjoy a clean, intuitive interface with dark mode support for comfortable browsing.

Our mission is to make information more accessible and understandable by combining the best of human-curated content with AI-powered insights.

Help Center

Searching with Aayushi AI

Enter your query in the search box and press Enter or click the search button.
Use the tabs above the search box to switch between Web and Image search.
Click on trending topics to quickly search popular subjects.

AI Features

The AI summary at the top provides a concise answer to your query.
For complex questions, the AI can provide more detailed explanations.
The AI learns from your searches to provide better results over time.

Settings & Preferences

Toggle between light and dark mode using the theme button in the header.
Adjust search settings like safe search and results per page in Settings.
Change your preferred language and region in Settings.
